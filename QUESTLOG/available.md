## Build-Specific Quests

See `train` or `dojo` for quests specific to your current build.

Each build has training quests in their dojo:
- Wizard: First Manifestation, Long Conversation, Spellbook
- Warrior: First Order, Failed Battle, Night Watch
- Ranger: Speed Run, The Forge
- Paladin: Founding Document, Grand Design
- Rogue: One-Liner, Automation, Shadow Clone
